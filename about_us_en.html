ABOUT US

Termez University of Economics and Service

An Academic Profile

Termez University of Economics and Service (TUES) is a dynamic and forward-looking higher education institution in Uzbekistan, strategically located in Termez, a city that historically functioned as a cultural and intellectual crossroads of Central Asia. As a private university dedicated to excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement, TUES is committed to preparing future leaders in economics, business, the social sciences, applied sciences, and professional service industries. Since its establishment, the university has strived to combine rigorous academic programs with practice-oriented training, while simultaneously cultivating a global vision through partnerships with distinguished institutions worldwide.


TUES now stands as one of the region’s most innovative educational institutions, recognized for integrating local and international perspectives in its curricula, advancing sustainable development practices, and providing its students with opportunities to participate in the international academic community. It is in this spirit of global intellectual exchange that the university hosts the international student symposium entitled “The World in 2050”, where students, academics, and professionals converge to explore challenges and opportunities shaping humanity’s shared future.



Academic Mission and Vision

The central mission of TUES is to nurture critical, creative, and globally competent graduates capable of contributing to the socio-economic transformation of Uzbekistan and beyond. The university’s vision is rooted in three guiding principles:


  1. Excellence in Education: Providing high-quality, research-driven, and interdisciplinary programs across economics, business, management, social sciences, linguistics, and applied fields.
  2. Global Integration: Promoting cross-cultural understanding and knowledge transfer through international partnerships and exchange initiatives.
  3. Sustainable Development: Advancing innovation in renewable energy, digital transformation, and community-oriented services that address pressing societal needs.

Through these principles, TUES not only equips its students with the necessary academic knowledge but also instills a sense of social responsibility and entrepreneurial spirit, preparing them to respond to complex global challenges.



Academic Programs and Facilities

TUES offers a diverse range of academic programs that reflect the growing demands of both local and international labor markets. Its core disciplines include economics, management, finance, business administration, linguistics, and information technology, supported by specialized training in service industries. Moreover, the university integrates practical experience into its academic structure. For example, TUES operates its own medical clinic, enabling students in medical and service-oriented fields to acquire hands-on skills while simultaneously serving local communities.


Research and data-driven decision-making are further supported by the university’s access to advanced facilities such as the Bloomberg Terminal, a tool widely used in leading global universities and financial institutions. This infrastructure enables faculty and students to engage in real-time economic forecasting, market analysis, and applied research projects with direct relevance to both industry and public policy.


Additionally, the university invests heavily in sustainable energy initiatives. Its solar energy infrastructure not only contributes to reducing environmental impact but also allows the university to supply clean energy to regional institutions, positioning TUES as a pioneer of green transformation within Uzbekistan’s higher education landscape.
