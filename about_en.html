About the Symposium

The International Student Symposium titled “The World in 2050” is a forward-looking academic event organized by Termiz University of Economics and Services. This two-day conference invites students from around the world to explore the trends, innovations, and challenges that will define our global society by the mid-21st century.

This global academic event invites students from universities around the world to explore and present ideas, research, and visions that address the major questions of our shared future. As we approach 2050, societies everywhere face unprecedented technological, environmental, social, and economic transformations. The aim of the symposium is to provide a platform for young voices to contribute to global conversations and solutions that will shape the decades to come.

We believe the future must be innovative, sustainable, and inclusive. Through interdisciplinary dialogue and international collaboration, this symposium empowers students to take part in imagining and building the world they want to live in.


In an age of rapid technological transformation, environmental uncertainty, and shifting global dynamics, this symposium offers a platform for young scholars and researchers to contribute their ideas and visions for a sustainable, inclusive, and innovative future.

The event aims to foster cross-cultural exchange, interdisciplinary dialogue, and student-led research that addresses real-world issues affecting the future of humanity.


Conference Vision

Aim

The International Student Symposium “The World in 2050” aims to foster interdisciplinary and international dialogue among students worldwide, empowering them to explore, envision, and contribute solutions for the complex challenges and opportunities facing humanity in the mid-21st century. By promoting innovation, sustainability, and global collaboration, the conference seeks to inspire future leaders and thinkers who will shape a better, inclusive, and resilient world.

Scope

The conference welcomes research, ideas, and projects addressing the multifaceted dimensions of the future world by 2050. It covers a broad range of themes including emerging technologies, environmental sustainability, transformations in education and employment, health and social wellbeing, global mobility, and inclusive economic growth. This wide scope encourages contributions from diverse academic disciplines and cultural perspectives, supporting a holistic approach to envisioning the future.

Objectives

  • Encourage student participation from around the world in a global academic dialogue on future challenges and innovations.
  • Promote interdisciplinary research and collaboration that bridges technology, environment, society, economy, and culture.
  • Raise awareness of the critical importance of sustainable development, ethical innovation, and equitable growth for the future.
  • Provide a platform for sharing cutting-edge ideas and best practices related to AI, climate action, health, education, tourism, and economic inclusion.
  • Foster international partnerships and networks among students, academics, and professionals to support ongoing cooperation beyond the conference.
  • Inspire action and leadership among young scholars to actively contribute to shaping policies and innovations that will define the world in 2050.
